Now this is a story all about how my life got flipped, turned upside down.

There was once a girl and her dog, who lived happily in a small hamlet called Crosshill. Nothing could separate the two, or so they thought... COLLEGE!!!! The dreaded post-secondary education tore our faithful friends away. The world was seemingly over for both parties.

The journey to see each other was a treacherous one for our young heroine, one filled with many beast, such as Newmarket at rush hour.
